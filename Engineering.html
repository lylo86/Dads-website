Our Vision

MARCO TECH (PTY) LTD, although registered in 2015, within its Directorship, has in excess of forty years diversely acquired experience to satisfy Client needs and will only use Qualified & Experience Personnel in the execution of those needs. Marco Tech (Pty) Ltd strives to satisfy Social Responsibility, by inducting aspiring young people into its ranks in terms of the Skills Development Act, thereby identifying talent in various fields and advancing this talent within their chosen career path.

Our Mision

MARCO TECH (PTY) LTD, Domiciled in Cape Town, seeks to service Clients within the Western Cape and certain Sectors of the Northern Cape, with focus on Municipalities, Corporates and Mining Concerns.

The Director, being extremely sensitive to the Vision & Mission of its Clients, Marco Tech (Pty) Ltd will Employ its many years of diversely acquired understanding and experience in the importance of People, Processes & Products in its execution of Commitment to Clients` needs.
