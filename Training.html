Our Mision

THE DIRECTOR OF MARCO TECH (PTY) LTD is a Registered Skills Development Facilitator, Assessor and Moderator, Constituent with the MERSETA and CHIETA, Sector Education and Training Authorities with the designation of Subject Matter Expert in his FIELD and further endorsed by the QCTO (Quality Council on Trades and Occupations).

With forty plus years of experience in the Engineering and Construction Sectors it is therefore the Company`s mission to impart this knowledge and experience to aspiring Trades People through direct consultation with the Business owners thereby conducting a Training and Compliance Needs Analysis and subsequently making recommendations based on the Analysis.

Ultimately the upskilling and qualification of the workforce is of paramount importance and this is achieved by monitoring, advising & mentoring and finally Qualification or Part Qualification via RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) or Training Interventions.

Services Offered

  • Company Policies & Procedures.
  • Occupational Health & Safety Act (OHSA) No. 85 of 1993 Compliance
  • Workplace Skills Analysis
  • Recommendations based on the Skills Analysis
  • Workplace Skills Plan
  • On the Job Mentoring / Training and Workshop or Site
  • Compilation of Method Statements and Risk Analysis.
  • Company Health and Safety Files
  • Recommendations on Construction Regulations in Conjunction with OHSA 85/93
  • RPL Assessment and Portfolio of Evidence (POE) compilation with recommendations for Part or Full Qualification.
